The Sarnoff Collection at TCNJ, S.572.33

spacewar-2.st2
==============
Space war 2 includes Studio II Space War (press 1) but the vertical intercept is started on key press 2 instead of 3. Otherwise they look very similar. The code is similar to Space War.

spacewar-3.st2
==============
Space war 3 includes Studio II Space War (press 1) but the vertical intercept is started on key press 2 instead of 3. Otherwise they look very similar but the code is very different from Space War or Space war 1.
