CAR RACE 

In this program you steer a car to the left and right to dodge the other cars which come down the screen at you. 

To one side is your fuel gauge which shows how much fuel you have left. Once you run out you can go on to your next car. 

After five cars your score is shown. To play use keys ‘4’ and ’6’. 

To load this program use 0480 at 0400 and 08FF at 0402.
