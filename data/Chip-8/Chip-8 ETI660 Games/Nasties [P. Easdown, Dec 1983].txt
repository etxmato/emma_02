NASTIES 

This is a game of speed. The aim of the game is to run over’ all of the black dots with your flashing dot, as fast as possible. 

At the bottom of the screen your score is shown along with the time left. The maximum time is about two minutes and twenty seconds. 

To move your dot use these keys: 4 — left; 6 — right; 8 — up; 2 — down. 

To load this program use 0600 at 0400 and 07FF at 0402.
