
=====================
 "Snafu" for Chip8:
=====================
 
 Author: Shendo
 Date: 2010

========
 About:
========

 Snafu is a clone of the Intellivision game by the same name.
 The goal of the game is to block your opponent.
 In order to win you need to score 10 points.

===========
 Controls:
===========

To move player (on a hexpad):

5 to go UP
7 to go Left
8 to go Down
9 to go right
