8CE 8TTORNEY: (Disk 1, 2 and 3)

The most expensive CHIP8 game made, taking up multiple game 'disks' to tell the story of the 
Lawyer Bell defending a courtroom janitor from murder charges. 

Controls:
---------
W (4) - Advance Text
Q (5) - (In Cross Exam) Press
E (6) - (In Cross Exam) Show Evidence
A (7) - Move Left in Evidence
D (9) - Move Right in Evidence
