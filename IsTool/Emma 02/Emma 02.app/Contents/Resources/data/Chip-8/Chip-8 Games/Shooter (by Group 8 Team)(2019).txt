Goal: Shoot a bullet that hits the other enemy!



The walls impede your way, so shoot them to remove them!



Controls:



Player 1:
1 key: Move up

4 key: Move left

5 key: Move right

7 key: Move down

8 Key: Shoot



Player 2:

C key: Move up

6 key: Move left

D key: Move right

E key: Move down

9 Key: Shoot
