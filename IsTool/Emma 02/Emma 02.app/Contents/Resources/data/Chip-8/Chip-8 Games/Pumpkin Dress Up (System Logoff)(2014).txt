PUMPKIN "DREESS" UP:

Welcome to Chip8Grounds, where simple flash games are popular and everyone makes them. Today 
we have a classic dress up game from the 1998-2002 internet period where you dress up a pumpkin 
in funny faces.

Controls:
Press Z to change Accessories
Press X to change Eyes
Press C to change mouths
(A/0/B) on octo keypad.

Note:
Typo on title screen placed to invoke the Newgrounds "feel".
