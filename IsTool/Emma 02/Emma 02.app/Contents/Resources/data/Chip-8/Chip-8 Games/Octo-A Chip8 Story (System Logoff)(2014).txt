You're a developer interested in making games, and you see the Octojam starting up. You decide to 
make a game, but the question is-- how will it turn out?

Controls:
Press Z/X/C when a choice appears.
For the Chip8 Keyboard: A/0/B
Any key will advance non-choice screens.

Goal:
Try and find all five endings! Each time you find an ending, a star will be added to the title 
screen!
