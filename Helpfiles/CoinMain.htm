RCA Video Coin Arcade Emulator

This emulator will emulate the RCA Video Coin Arcade computer. This emulator is based on input I received from Andy Modla and his emulator as can be found on https://github.com/ajavamind/rca-studio2.

To start the RCA Video Coin Arcade emulator first select the Studio tab (yellow ellipse) followed by the RCA Video Coin Arcade tab (orange ellipse) and then press Start:
