ETI 660 Emulator

This emulator will emulate the ETI 660 computer. Additional information about the ETI 660 can be found on the Emma 02 site.

After starting the ETI 660 emulator the 'F12' key can be used to simulate the reset button and the 'Insert', '+' or 'numpad +' to simulate the step button. See also Function and Hot Keys.
